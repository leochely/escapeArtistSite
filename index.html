What is Escape Artist?


You are trapped in your canvas. Try to escape.


Escape Artist is a school project made by 4 computer science students. The game has been made with Game Maker Studio 2. This is a semester long project that ended up being featured at the Colorado School of Mines Spring 2019 Innovation Fair.
